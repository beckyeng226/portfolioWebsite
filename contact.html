About Me

In the spring of 2019, I decided to change career directions and become a programmer. I spent several months taking online courses trying to narrow down what I wanted to do. After taking several HTML/CSS courses, I muddled through the design and development of my first website. I learned a LOT, including how much I didn't know, but I also enjoyed it tremendously. I decided to continue expanding my learning to include JavaScript, and I'm currently working my way through the Full-Stack Developer Path at Codecademy, which I plan to complete in the Spring of 2021.

When I leave my laptop, I enjoy outdoor activities like hiking, camping, and biking. I train and teach at an all-female karate dojo, where I am working towards my black belt. The geek in me loves to assemble puzzles (Star Wars Photomosaics are my favorite challenge), solve word and math puzzles/games, and read. I also enjoy knitting and crocheting to relax.
